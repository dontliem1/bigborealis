Genius Immersive Experience

The Genius Immersive Experience is a creative journey through the eyes of Leonardo da Vinci, as if he were alive today. It’s like nothing you’ve experienced ever before

Genius is not just
another show

It’s a completely new, immersive art experience that sets the tone for the next generation of shows

Next-generation. Just as Leonardo da Vinci was ahead of his time, so too is this show — pushing the boundaries of visual content to astonishing effect. Prepare to discover something truly original.

Touchable & Playable. What’s it like to be inside one of the brightest minds that ever existed? Interact and feel the power of human creativity, the light — bulb moment of invention and the cognitive workings of a Genius.

Edutainment. Revisit Leonardo’s inventions and ideas within a modern framework. Find new meanings in them, reflect on your own talents and endeavours, and take a glimpse into the future.

Music Experience. Take a moment to slow down, focus on the now and tune into your thoughts. Give yourself up to the frisson-inducing musical score and interactive visuals that accompany the show, within a 360-degree soundscape.

Concept

The Big Bang was the beginning of everything. The planets and the universe were born with it. But why was it all created if there was no one to feel it, to experience it, and to discover? There was one part of the puzzle missing…

The Genius: Leonardo explored the world around him through science, art and intuition. Humankind was not created just to mark time, he realised, but also to invent, create and aspire to achieve knowledge.

«There are three classes of people: those who see, those who see when they are shown, those who do not see», — Leonado Da Vinci

Almost 80 million real pixels, 4 km of cabling and more than 350,000 projected lumens come together to create a sensory riot. In this revision of Leonardo’s work, the Genius Immersive Experience presents what his vision might have been today. How would he perceive our attitudes towards ecology, nature, science, space exploration and modern art? Discover the answers to our future by examining our past.

This is an absolute revision of Da Vinci's work, minding modern realities: ecology problems, the discovery of outer space, new meanings and trends in modern art, and the seach for the human kind’s mission in this world.

«All our knowledge has its origins in our perceptions», — Leonado Da Vinci

Team

The Genius Immersive Show is produced by Borealis Interactive Experimental, whose team is focused on the next generation of immersive shows.

We create a brand-new experience to the experience industry and strive to bring the viewer to a fundamentally higher level of emotion ever by using modern technologies and engaging the best design teams.

Our mission is to help people revise the heritage and influence of great minds, events, and inventions to humanity.

We believe that revision of the past can provide answers to the questions and challenges humanity is dealing with right now. By bringing together people of different countries, races, ages, genders and backgrounds we aspire to unite and to give another chance to rediscover ourselves.
